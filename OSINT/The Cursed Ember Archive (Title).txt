The Cursed Ember Archive



Description: 
Long before the dragon revealed itself, warnings were whispered across the realms by an informant known only as Northflame.

These warnings were gathered into the Ember Archive — a cursed collection of scrolls where messages repeat, contradict, and drown the truth in smoke.

The archivists failed to determine where Northflame truly was when the dragon awakened.

You are adventurers on a quest for flame and glory.
Your task is to cut through the noise and uncover the truth.

Determine the realm and kingdom where Northflame witnessed the dragon’s awakening on the 14th Day of the Ember Moon.

The archive is vast and misleading.
Perhaps a thinking flame can burn away what does not matter.
